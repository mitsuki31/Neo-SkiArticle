# Code of Conduct

## Purpose

This website represents the school and its community. To keep it safe, respectful, and aligned with the school’s values, all visitors and contributors are expected to follow this **Code of Conduct**. These rules apply to all interactions involving the site, including submitted reports, feedback, or feature requests.

## Expected Behavior

Participants should:

- Communicate respectfully with all individuals.
- Use the website responsibly and avoid actions that disrupt its operation.
- Respect privacy and handle school-related information with care.
- Follow the [Security Policy](./SECURITY.md) when reporting vulnerabilities.
- Understand that this website exists for educational purposes and school information — not for data harvesting or content redistribution.

## Protection of School Information

All users agree to:

- **Not copy, repost, redistribute, or publish content** from this website—especially content involving students, activities, or school operations—without explicit permission from the school.
- **Not collect personal data** from this site for any unauthorized use.
- **Not attempt to bypass, scrape, or automate** access to information beyond normal browsing.

These rules exist to protect students and maintain a safe online presence.

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behavior will not be tolerated:

- Harassment, discrimination, or disrespectful conduct toward anyone associated with the school.
- Sharing, leaking, or misusing information obtained from the website.
- Attempting to access restricted areas, exploiting vulnerabilities, or performing unauthorized testing.
- Spamming, trolling, or introducing harmful content.

## Reporting Behavior Issues

If you notice behavior violating this **Code of Conduct**:

- For sensitive or private matters, use the confidential contact information in `/.well-known/security.txt`.
- For general website misuse or content concerns, open a public GitHub issue with appropriate context (no personal data).

For more information, please refer to the [Security page](https://neoski.vercel.app/security).

## Enforcement

The maintainers may take necessary actions, including:

- Removing inappropriate content.
- Restricting or blocking access to the website or its features.
- Reporting serious violations to school administration or relevant authorities.

Decisions are made at the maintainers’ discretion based on the severity of the situation.

## Scope

This **Code of Conduct** applies to:

- All pages and features of the website.
- All contributions, reports, discussions, and interactions related to the site.

## Agreement

By using, interacting with, or contributing to this website, you agree to follow this Code of Conduct.
